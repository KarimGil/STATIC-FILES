Top 10 Best Budget Laptops Under $200

Apr 02, 2020 by Mike Johnson in Budget Laptops

Gone are the days when you had to spend a hell lot of money to buy a good laptop because now many laptop manufacturers are offering decent performing laptops for the price lesser than $200! They are best for basic everyday tasks, but still, there are many tasks which these cheap laptops can’t perform. So which are the best laptops under $200? Are the laptops under 200 dollars really worth buying? Let’s find it out.

Of course, while buying the budget laptop, you have to keep your expectations in check, but when the budget is $200, you really have to take that seriously!

You can’t expect to play the newest games or perform demanding tasks such as video editing on these budget laptops, because the Windows Netbooks and Chromebooks that you would usually find in this price range are simply not made for it. They run on the lightweight operating systems such as Windows 10 S and Chrome OS and are powered by an entry-level CPUs paired with very less amount of RAM and storage which although can’t handle demanding workloads, they are perfect for everyday productivity tasks such as surfing the web, editing documents, managing small excel sheets and creating presentations. They also come in a very small and lightweight form factor, and as its less powerful hardware consumes very less power, most of them have good battery life as well.

So, they can be a great option for frequent travelers, students, or anyone who wants an inexpensive laptop for answering emails, editing documents, and other such basic tasks. However, for anything more than that, the sub-$200 laptops will be way underpowered. If you were expecting more from these budget notebooks, then we strongly recommend you to increase your budget and check out other budget laptop buying guides on our website which includes more capable laptops that cost just slightly more than $200.

Well, if you think that these budget laptops are what you have been looking for, then as I said earlier, while searching for the best cheap laptops in the market, you will see there are mostly Windows laptops and Chromebooks available to buy under $200 budget. So, the first thing which you have to decide is whether you should buy a Windows laptop or a Chromebook?

Windows Laptop or Chromebook: What should you buy under $200?

To buy the best and reliable laptop within your limited budget, you have to decide whether Windows laptops are best for your daily needs or Chromebooks. Below are a few comparisons which shall give you a proper idea of what is Chromebook and how it is different from Windows laptop that you’re probably used to. Go through it and then take your decision for buying the best cheap laptop under 200 dollars for yourself.

If you already know what you’re looking for then feel free to skip this and begin with the list of our top laptop picks below.

  • As we all know Windows is the most used operating system on computers so getting used to a new windows laptop will be no problem for most of us. Whereas, Chrome OS, on the other hand, is still found on very limited laptops. While getting used to it won’t be much problem, as its UI is almost similar to using Chrome on Windows laptop but for those who don’t use Chrome, it can be a bit of work.
  • There are no software compatibility problems on Windows laptops. You can download apps and games from Microsoft Store and even install software from other third-party sources. On the other hand, Chromebooks are still limited to apps in the Chrome web store. Google added the Android Play Store support to the Chrome OS and many older Chromebooks did get the software update for that, but it is still available on the selected models only. Moreover, the apps on Play Store aren’t fully optimized for the Chromebooks, so if you are planning to use the android apps on your Chromebook, then make sure to get the touch-enabled one.
  • If you are looking for a cheap laptop to work on the MS Office, then definitely Windows laptop is the only option, as Chrome OS doesn’t support it yet. However, if you rely on Google’s suite of applications heavily and have to work online mostly, then nothing can beat Chromebooks at that. Chromebooks are also fast and battery efficient than Windows laptops as Chrome OS is a very lightweight cloud-based operating system.

There are many more differences between them, but these are the major ones. You can check out this guide on Chromebooks vs Windows Laptops for more in-depth idea about it. Keep your needs in mind and then decide whether you should buy a Chromebook under $200 or Windows laptop under $200.

So, now as you have decided what type of laptop you want to buy for yourself, we can proceed with the list of best laptops to buy under your budget of 200 dollars.

Note -
All budget laptops under $200 come with a very little amount of internal storage (64GB at most) so to buy a laptop with more storage (500GB or 1TB HDD), we recommend you to increase your budget a bit and have a look at our guide to buy the best laptop under $300. With little extra money, you can buy a much more powerful laptop than these windows netbooks and Chromebooks.
