Top 10 Best Intel Core i3 Processor Laptops

Mar 28, 2020 by Mike Johnson in Best of Best

The hardware of the laptop is one of the most crucial things to check before buying any laptop and processor which is known as a brain of the computer, is the most crucial thing to check in hardware specifications of any laptop because it decides overall performance and capabilities of the laptop. When it comes to computer processors, Intel has been the greatest contender since very long time. So, in this post, we have got the top 10 best Intel core i3 processor laptops which will help you to buy the best i3 laptop for yourself within your budget.

Intel i3 Processor Laptops i.e. “Uncompromised performance at compromised price.”

The above-stated maxim is totally true because,

Intel Core i3 processor laptops are the most affordable and value for money laptops which are best for performing day to day tasks like internet surfing, editing word documents, making presentations, watching movies, multitasking and even moderate gaming.

But,

Intel i3 processor is designed to handle basic day to day works. Yes, you can run softwares like Adobe Photoshop and play games like Counter-Strike on these core i3 laptops but if you are planning to do more of it, then we recommend you to check out best Intel core i5 laptops because i3 processor laptops aren’t powerful enough for doing demanding tasks like photo editing, video making or hardcore gaming.

Now, as you have a clear idea about the performance of Intel i3 processor, you can easily decide whether Intel i3 processor laptops will be best for your everyday work or not.

So, if you have decided to buy the core i3 laptop for yourself then below are our top 10 picks from all available laptops in the market to buy the best i3 laptop.
