The Best Laptops For College Students of 2019 – For Every Budget and College Majors

Aug 13, 2019 by Mike Johnson in Best of Best

We are living in a modern era wherein almost all our tasks are handled and assisted by a computer. The same applies to students as well, as they have to carry a portable computer a.k.a. laptop to the college for their assistance. However, the requirements of all students are not similar as it depends upon the college major and classes they take. Also, everyone is bound to budget, which is often limited in this case, as most of it is occupied by tuition, accommodation, and other college expenses (that never ends). So, to pick the best laptop for college is not an easy task, and students often take the wrong decision of buying a laptop, which is not the best available for their money. We have simplified the process and have brought you the best available college laptops of 2019 in various segments of price with compatible features and hardware for students studying in different college majors.

We have set up this guide budget-wise by picking up the best laptops for college students across all the price ranges. So, you’ll find a laptop that costs under $200 in this guide and even the one that costs $2000. However, we would like to give you guys a little disclaimer that if you are taking a major that requires powerful specs, then don’t look at any laptops priced under $400 in this guide as they are only suitable for basic productivity tasks. Of course, there are few exclusions such as Acer Aspire 5 and ASUS VivoBook 15, which are an excellent value machines for less than $400 but other than that most of them are Chromebooks and Windows netbooks which are only good for surfing the web and working with Office apps. While they might not be great for all college students, but it’s an excellent option for students taking online classes or for high school students.

So, check out our top picks of laptops that are ideal for college students below and pick the right one in accordance with your college major and budget.

However, if you’re looking for the best tablet to take to the classroom, you can check out our buying guide for tablets for college students on our sister site Tablet Under Budget.
