Storage department on the laptop is managed by the small yet speedy 128GB of SSD. It has Intel UHD integrated graphics which offers adequate graphics back up to run Adobe Photoshop and other similar software efficiently.

Because of the latest specs and features that ASUS VivoBook 15 offers, it is one of the biggest contenders in the best laptops under $400 segment.
