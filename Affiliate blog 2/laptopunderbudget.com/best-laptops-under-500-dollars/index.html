Top 10 Best Laptops Under $500

Jan 10, 2020 by Mike Johnson in All Purpose Laptops

We generally research on laptops at Laptop Under Budget to find the best laptops from a lot and suggest to our audience, but this time we did something different. We tried to find out what is the most common budget among the people when they are looking for a new laptop. To do so, we checked thousands of expert advice requests that we receive every month through this Get Expert Advice page on our website. To our surprise, we found that $500 is the most common budget among the people and for that price people wanted the laptops that can perform all the everyday tasks and also some light gaming. Some of them were also concerned about a good display while some of them wanted a lightweight laptop with full day battery life and a good keyboard, all under 500 dollars.

Finding such laptops is a challenging task because sub-$500 laptops are still considered to be the budget category laptops (i.e., low-performance laptops), and there are tons of laptops in the market to easily puzzle the person.

We took this into account and worked accordingly, we tested a lot of laptops from different manufacturers in 500 dollars price range and finally here’s the outcome of it — A list of best laptops under $500 of 2020. We picked all the best all-purpose laptops in $500 price range which we think are the best laptops to suit the vast array of requirements of people.

So let’s have a look at all the best budget all-purpose laptops one by one.

NOTE – Most laptops in this guide are ranging in between $400 to $500. If you are looking for even cheaper laptops, then you can check out our budget laptop buying guides where you will find laptops priced under $400 and lesser price ranges than that.
