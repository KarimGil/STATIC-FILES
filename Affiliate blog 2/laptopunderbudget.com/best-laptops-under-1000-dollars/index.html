Top 10 Best Laptops Under $1000

Jan 09, 2020 by Sukesh Mudrakola in All Purpose Laptops

In the present day scenario, everyone irrespective of the age, qualification, and profession needs a laptop. Ever since the market has witnessed a growing demand for laptops, there have been companies emerging with the latest laptop models at cut-throat prices. Although these growing laptops in the market have considerably reduced the prices, it has paved the way for an even more complicated issue of choosing the best laptop from the lot. That said, there are hundreds of laptops available in $1000 price range. Now, which is the best laptop under $1000? What configuration is right for your requirements and optimal for the money you are going to spend? These are the questions arriving in everyone’s mind.

We here at laptopunderbudget.com have come up with a soothing solution for the above queries. We have hand-picked some of the best laptops under $1000 price tag. Laptops in this price range are beasts, and they can do almost anything you throw at it but are…

“Laptops Under $1000 – Best for Gaming?”

The answer to this question is: Yes! With $1000 budget, you can get a powerful gaming laptop with latest six-core Intel processor, powerful dedicated Nvidia GPU and fast SSD storage which is enough to run all the latest games in medium to high graphics settings. So definitely, sub-$1000 laptops are best for gaming and ultimately for other tasks like producing music, light video editing, CAD work and everything else in between.

The below-given list contains some gaming laptops under 1000 dollars but as this is an “all-purpose laptop” buying guide, it has laptops which are all-rounders in all forms. If you are looking for the laptop which is built from scratch for gaming only, then we have a separate guide on “gaming laptops under $1000” which includes our top 10 recommendations of gaming laptops under your budget of one thousand dollars.

However, if you are looking for an all-purpose laptop which provides the perfect mixture of performance and portability, and the one that allows you to do everything without any limitations then go through the complete list of laptops below and pick the one in accordance with your necessity.

2. Apple MacBook Air (Newest Model)

Apple MacBook Air is undoubtedly the perfect blend of elegant design, all-metal aluminum chassis, long-lasting battery backup, and powerful configuration. Although there are a really large number of laptops based on Windows and even Linux in $1000 price range, MacBook Air still holds a firm position in market share because of its sturdy design and reliable performance.

The 2019 MacBook Air comes with an 8th generation Intel Core i5 processor which has clock frequency of up to 3.6GHz. The processor is backed by an 8GB of RAM which takes care of the multitasking and it offers 128GB of SSD for storage which is less but there are options for 256GB and 512GB as well (and they cost a lot). It comes preloaded with macOS Mojave and all other standard macOS apps which makes it ready to work out of the box. The laptop doesn’t come with an integrated fan for the CPU’s cooling, and thus consumes very less power and is very power efficient. Thus, it is evident that the latest MacBook Air is capable enough to outmuscle most of its competitors when it comes to battery life.

However, not everything about the new MacBook Air is praiseworthy. Just like the MacBook Pro, the new MacBook Air now comes with USB Type-C Thunderbolt ports only, so you’ll have to buy dongles if you want standard USB ports or an SD card reader. Moreover, the keyboard on the new MacBook Air has also been updated with the new butterfly mechanism switches which has very less travel. The laptop is also priced slightly on the higher side than its competitors, but all in all the new MacBook Air is still worthy enough because of its reliable performance and full-day battery life.
