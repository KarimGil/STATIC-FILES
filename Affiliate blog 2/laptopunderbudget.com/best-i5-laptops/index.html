Top 10 Best Intel Core i5 Processor Laptops

Aug 20, 2019 by Sukesh Mudrakola in Best of Best

Intel is serving laptop market with its powerful range of processors since very long time. Intel processor laptops are not only best in terms of performance, but they are budget friendly as well. Among all of them, the Intel i5 Processor laptops are best affordable high-performance machines which will let you perform almost all types of tasks. So to help you out in buying the best i5 laptop, I have got here a list of top 10 best i5 processor laptops which includes best Intel Core i5 laptops, notebooks, 2-in-1s and also gaming laptops…

Technology has taken tremendous leaps when it comes to computing devices. The computers, which are essentially meant to reduce human efforts were first developed in the size of a huge room. However, the size is one key factor which has been drastically minimized. The size-stripping has now resulted in the present generation’s laptops which are essentially small but inversely powerful. That power is mostly because of laptop’s processor so the processor is one of the most crucial things to check while buying a new laptop.

Well if you have made your mind to buy the Intel i5 processor laptop only, then here are some of the top picks from a wide array of options available when it comes to buying the best i5 laptop…

1. Acer Aspire E 15

Speaking of the external appearance of the laptop, the Acer Aspire has made its new mid-range flagship laptop with an appealing yet well-engineered design. The laptop sports an obsidian black colored body and comes with a brushed aluminum lid. The laptop comes with a 15.6-inch Full HD LED-backlit display, which is very bright and provides crisp resolution. Additionally, Acer Aspire E 15 is one among those very few laptops which offer the backlit keyboard at this price.

Under the hood, the laptop is powered by an Intel i5-8250U processor coupled with 8GB of DDR4 RAM. The device houses the new Kaby Lake-R processor from Intel which is a quad-core chip and is known to provide the best balance of performance and battery life. The laptop is ideal for professional usage as well as multimedia usage. That said, the laptop can be used as a gaming machine all thanks to its Nvidia GeForce MX150 with 2GB of dedicated graphics memory. In terms of storage, it packs 256GB of internal storage and its SSD so a lot faster than hard drives.

“Acer Aspire E 15” is also featured as the best laptop for students in our guide to buy the laptop for college of 2019.

2. Dell G5 15 Best i5 Gaming Laptop

When it comes to a powerful performance protected by a sturdy built, Dell stands out to be a clear winner. And accordingly, the 2019 Dell G5 15 creeps at number two in the list of best i5 laptops. The laptop is powered by a 9th Gen Intel Core i5 processor clocked at 2.4GHz and turbo up to 4.1GHz. The machine comes preloaded with Windows 10 and also boasts the license to most of Dell’s lineup of laptop’s applications.

For handling multiple apps, G5 15 is provided with an 8GB of RAM, which can be further expanded up to 32GB. That said, the laptop comes with an additional slot for RAM expansion. Its Nvidia GeForce GTX 1660 Ti GPU stands out as the most popular dedicated graphics card in the segment, and thus provides an excellent gaming experience on it. It flaunts a Full HD 15.6-inch IPS display, which can taste both the extremes of brightness.

Coming to the internal storage of the laptop, this particular variant of Dell G5 comes preloaded with 1TB of HDD and 128GB of SSD, which should be enough for most users. Although the laptop is categorized under gaming genre, it is undoubtedly capable of providing an impressive multimedia and business class productivity.

3. Apple MacBook Air

When it comes to productivity and reliable battery life, no other brands till now have come any closer to the tech-giant Apple’s MacBook series of laptops. It is styled in a complete aluminum casing, which is provided with a smooth metal finish. The laptop looks premium and is even more thinner and lighter than the previous MacBook Air at a mere 0.61-inches and 2.7 pounds respectively.

The new MacBook Air runs macOS Mojave out of the box and comes preloaded with a host of useful apps. It is powered by a Y-series Intel Core i5 processor coupled with 8GB of RAM and 128GB SSD. It’s an ample example to explain that specifications aren’t everything, but it is the mutual working between hardware and software of a device which makes it efficient.

Though the laptop is not as powerful as its competitors in the list, it is surely capable of outlasting most laptops with i5 processor when it comes to productivity. Speaking of productivity, the new MacBook Air for the first time comes with the Retina display, new butterfly mechanism keyboard with Touch ID and a large trackpad similar to the MacBook Pro laptops from Apple. 

It is a bit pricey, though. It starts at $1099 and for the price you get a very basic set of specifications. Although it can be configured with more RAM and storage, those upgrades adds up a lot to the price.

4. HP Envy 17t 17.3-Inch Intel i5 Processor Laptop

Ever wondered about having a bigger laptop for a bigger and better performance? If yes, then surely the HP Envy 17t with a huge 17.3-inch screen will not disappoint you in any manner. It runs on a quad-core Intel Core i5 processor clocked at 1.6GHz, which can overclock up to 3.9GHz. The laptop runs on Windows 10, which is the latest version of Windows yet. Its 17.3-inch full HD display is provided with an energy-efficient LED backlight, which is optimum for saving the power consumption.

An 8GB of DDR4 RAM handles the multitasking, and for storing the user data, it houses a 1TB of HDD with 16GB of Intel Optane memory to speed up everything. Connectivity wise, the laptop houses all the essential options such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, HDMI port, and USB ports. The laptop comes with dedicated Nvidia GeForce MX250 graphics, which is good for moderate gaming and best for everyday activities.

This 17-inch sized laptop is quite heavy and weighs 6.1 pounds. One must be convenient to adapt the size and weight to purchase this laptop. However, the laptop stands out to be an excellent all-around performer and is backed with a 3-cell 52 Wh Lithium-Ion battery which delivers battery backup of up to 5-hours.

5. Dell XPS 13 Ultrabook with 8th Generation Intel i5 Processor

Dell’s XPS lineup of laptops has always been one of the best premium Windows ultrabooks packed with the newest hardware and features. The 2018 Dell XPS 13 was one of our favorite Windows ultrabooks, but it had one major flaw. Its webcam was a nose peeker as it was located on the bottom bezel of the display. The latest 2019 Dell XPS 13 fixes this problem, and we think it is the perfect Windows ultrabook to date. It is made from the premium materials like carbon fiber, glass, and aluminum and comes in two svelte looking finishes, namely platinum silver and frost white. It is very thin and weighs less than 3 pounds but still manages to pack latest Whiskey Lake Intel Core i5 quad-core processor inside making it an equally capable ultrabook.

Its 13.3-inch full HD InfinityEdge display is a treat to the eyes, and it is also very bright and color-accurate panel. On the hardware front, it bolsters an 8GB of RAM and 256GB of SSD, which should be enough for most users. The ports, however, are very limited on the 2019 model, as it comes with only 3 USB Type-C ports and an SD card slot. While those Type-C ports do support thunderbolt connections, but to connect any traditional USB device you will need a dongle. Apart from that, there is no additional graphics card provided with the laptop and thus, can serve the gaming purpose to a limited extent. 

All in all, the Dell XPS 13 is still the best thin and light i5 powered ultrabook, which is powerful enough to be an excellent daily performer.
