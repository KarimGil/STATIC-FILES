]]> MSI are the latest in the long line of gaming laptop manufacturers to unveil their newest and latest RTX gaming laptops at CES 2019.

For 2019, MSI will usher in three new RTX gaming laptops into the market: the GS75 Stealth laptop, the PS63 Modern laptop, and the GE75 Raider laptop.

Each gaming laptop in their latest lineup is aimed towards catering to a specific target audience, but all three are made to help reaffirm MSI’s “position as an industry leader endorsed by gamers and eSports teams worldwide.”

Alongside their RTX gaming laptops, MSI also used CES 2019 to announce several of their latest products as well. This includes a new pre-built desktop, a new desktop case, a new gaming keyboard, and their newest line of high-performance graphics cards, spearheaded by the MSI GeForce RTX 2080 Ti GAMING X TRIO.

Say Hello to MSI’s RTX Gaming Laptops

Leading the charge for MSI’s newest RTX gaming laptops is the MSI GS75 Stealth laptop. Coming in at just 0.75 inches thick and sporting a thin bezel design, all housed in an aluminum alloy body for a premium look, the GS75 is meant to stand out aesthetically. Specs-wise, the GS75 doesn’t lag behind its competition either. It comes with a huge 17.3-inch 144Hz IPS-level display, the latest Intel Core i7 series CPUs, and perhaps more importantly, the NVIDIA RTX 2080 GPU.

MSI GS75 Stealth

The GS75’s smaller brother, the GS65, is also receiving an update for the new year as well. The newest version of the 15.6-inch gaming laptop that weighs just 4.19 pounds will include the best and latest CPUs and GPUs the market has to offer.

MORE: The Best Gaming Laptops Under $1500 – Our Top Picks

Next up is the PS63 Modern. A laptop built for another market entirely, it is not the same beast of a gaming laptop that the GS75 is. At just 0.63 inches thick and 3.53 pounds, the PS63 Modern is perfect for content creators who are looking for a lightweight laptop that can doesn’t lag behind in terms of performance. It comes equipped with up to an Intel Core i7 processor and up to an NVIDIA GTX 1050 GPU. More importantly, it has a 16-hour battery life and MSI’s own proprietary software called the “Creator Center” aimed specifically towards people who use creator software frequently.

Last, but definitely not the least, is the GE75 Raider gaming laptop. Much like the GS75, it comes equipped with the latest NVIDIA RTX 2080 GPU. However, different from the GS75, the GE75 will feature a thicker, more gamerish aesthetic, complete with a reflective diamond cut trim and a red anodized finish for a more aggressive look.

Pricing and Availability

Unfortunately, MSI didn’t unveil any details regarding when the general public will have a chance to get their hands on their latest RTX gaming laptops. However, many expect the Taiwan-based company to release more information regarding about the pricing and the availability of their RTX gaming laptops pretty soon.

Design

MSI is not just known for their powerful innards and gaming performance, the devices are globally-reputed for their beautifully crafted aesthetics and design which compliments gamers.

Speaking of our current device, the MSI GL62M 7REX uses the same case and design as most of its predecessors. The only difference being, the laptop is considerably slimmer and light weighed when compared to its predecessors.

Thin and Light for Gaming on-the-go

However, the lightweight and portability are achieved on the cost of an optical drive, which the device lacks. The body of the device comes with a matte-finished plastic chassis with a bright red accent on the rear, which goes all the way to the dual fan grilles. The device comes with the dimension 15.1 x 10.2 x 1.1 inches which is pretty much the norm for 15.6-inched laptops.

On an overall note, the design of the device is pretty stylish, compact, slim, and easy to carry. Moreover, the device gives you that perfect mean-for-gaming hue by all the means. With this laptop, the company seems to aim at achieving portability by attaining the slim chassis and at a thickness of 1.06 inches with weight just over 5.29 pounds, the MSI GL62M 7REX gaming laptop is extremely portable.

Display

The MSI GL62M comes with a 15.6″ Full HD eDP matte-finished display. The display on the devices holds a resolution of 1920-by-1080 pixels which is powered by In-Plane Switching (IPS) technology. It is due to this, the display provides wide-viewing angles.

Color Accurate and Easy on Eyes

The device comes with 94% NTSC color range, which means, the device is capable of delivering very competent color reproduction which makes this laptop also an excellent choice for photo editing. Of course, this better color gamut complements the gaming on MSI GL62M 7REX, which we personally experienced and liked. The blacks are punchy and the panel is bright enough even for outdoor usage. We did not feel any fatigue even after using the laptop for prolonged durations, which is an excellent thing to consider for the gaming laptops.

Hardware

MSI GL62M comes with a very powerful and able set of specifications and hardware, which bolsters its brand reputation. MSI, being one of the world-renowned gaming laptop manufacturers delivers powerful machines with futuristic tech to meet the audience’s requirements.

Under the hood, the device is powered by an Intel Core i7-7700HQ quad-core processor, which can be clocked at 2.8-3.8 GHz depending on the load on the processor. For multitasking and handling applications, the device comes with an 8GB of DDR4 RAM clocked at 2400MHz. The RAM is further expandable up to 32GB with the unused slot provided. For the graphics segment, the MSI GL62M 7REX comes with a 4GB of NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050Ti graphics processor, which runs the show on the laptop. All the games and other graphics-heavy applications are handled by the GPU with ease.

Audio & Video

The MSI GL62M’s audio is pumped by the company’s proprietary Nahimic 2 technology. This is an Audio Enhancement tech, which provides powerful audio without compromising on the details. During our review, the audio from the laptop generating was great. Especially, the depths of audio, while playing games made us feel the gaming environment around us. As far as the videos are concerned, thanks to its bright display and graphics abilities, the laptop can play all formats of video content with ease.

Battery Life

Battery life is vital. However, in the case of gaming laptops, battery life cannot be expected to provide any wonders. That said, the MSI GL62M lasted for about 4 hours on our rundown tests, which included continuous heavy usage and multitasking. Although this is pretty much what we get of a gaming laptop, we certainly expected that the battery would last longer at least by an hour. The addition of heat pipes and dual cooling systems might be the reason for the high battery usage.

The laptop takes about two and half hours for it to get completely charged form 0 to 100. Again, if we’re speaking about this in a gaming laptop’s perspective, then the MSI GL62M 7REX-1896US gaming laptop will satisfy most, if not all.

Conclusion

On a conclusive note, the laptop at this price is definitely a very decent buy. However, if you’re not a complete gamer, then we’d recommend you to switch to another other device in the price range to get a better RAM, and battery backup. All the gamers, need to keep in mind that this device is a mid-range gaming laptop to keep their expectations in check. That said, the laptop still has a lot of amazing specs and features such as a bright display, powerful audio, very sturdy performance, and amazing look and aesthetics.
