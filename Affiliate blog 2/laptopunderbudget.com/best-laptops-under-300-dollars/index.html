Top 10 Best Budget Laptops Under $300

Apr 03, 2020 by Mike Johnson in Budget Laptops

Nowadays you don’t need to break the bank to buy the best laptop because even budget laptops are more capable these days. Well if you are planning to spend $300 on your new laptop, then there are some of the most proficient laptops available to buy in this price range. Laptops under $300 are best for day to day works and also for moderate gaming! So which are the best laptops under 300 dollars? Are these budget laptops really capable of doing intensive tasks like gaming? Let’s find it out.

In this highly competitive laptop market where new laptops are launched after every few weeks, it is hard for people to buy the best laptop for their daily computing needs. However, for budget buyers, this has been no less than a boon because laptops which were launched just 5-6 months back are getting substantial price cuts due to newer laptops launched in the market! This creates an opportunity for budget buyers to buy more capable laptops at lower prices. Well if we talk about buying laptops under $300 budget specifically, then there are a lot of powerful laptops available to buy in this price range. You can efficiently perform all day to day works on these budget laptops and also do some intensive tasks like photo editing and even moderate gaming. But, you should not forget that…

Laptops under 300 dollars are budget laptops, i.e., low-performance laptops. They have got power to let you do intensive power demanding tasks but not as fast as high-end laptops can do! So you need to be very patient while doing photo or light video editing and other similar works on these budget laptops. Same goes for gaming as well. You won’t be able to play all the latest games, but there are few old games which run smoothly on these laptops.

1. Lenovo Ideapad 130

As we all know, Lenovo is one of the most trusted laptop brands when it comes to buying a reliable laptop in limited budget. Lenovo Ideapad 130 is also of the same kind. It is a lightweight, robust and durable laptop and it is ideal for business people because of its standard 15-inch form-factor and high-performance hardware.

The laptop is finished in gray color, and Lenovo branding on its lid adds professional looks to the laptop. Its 15.6 inches of HD LED display isn’t the best when it comes to resolution, but it is good enough for typical day to day office works. From its thickness, it seems like it is a bulky laptop, but it weighs just 4.6 pounds so traveling with this laptop wouldn’t be a problem. It has USB 3.0, USB 2.0, Ethernet and HDMI ports, and it also comes with an SD card slot so connectivity is a breeze on this laptop. Moreover, it also comes with an optical drive which can read and write CDs and DVDs.

For fulfilling all performance needs this laptop has an AMD A9-9425U dual-core processor with clock frequency of up to 3.7GHz which is coupled with 4GB of RAM. It generates enough power to multitask and run all the work related software without any lags. Moreover, you get 128GB of solid state drive storage, and it also has integrated AMD Radeon graphics, if you want to do some photo editing or gaming on this laptop.
